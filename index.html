The Rich List is a yearly ranking of the richest people in Quebec. The numbers are careful estimates based on public and private information. For anyone with shares in public companies, we used stock prices from June X, 2025. You can read more about how we made the list below.

Methodology

The Gazette Rich List 2025 estimates the minimum net worth of Quebec’s 25 richest individuals and families. Our goal is to give a clear, transparent snapshot of wealth that can be publicly identified and verified.
