Harry's Haul

The mission

Harry's Haul delivers clearly and immersively what matters most.

Settle in

Hi, I’m Harry North, a reporter at The Montreal Gazette. Each week, I’m out in the city — talking to people, chasing leads, and reporting on the ground.

But in the rush of daily headlines, one question often gets lost: when the dust settles, what actually matters?

That question drives Harry's Haul. It’s not a breaking-news feed. It’s a weekly, curated experience — built by me stepping back and asking which stories are most important, revealing, or simply delightful. The kind you screenshot and pass to friends. The kind that makes you think. And we're starting in Montreal.

The format

Each edition follows a rough structure:

5 Essentials. These are the stories that defined the week in Montreal.

3 Imports. Global developments with local relevance in Montreal.

2 Next Deliveries. Signals and shifts to watch in the week ahead.

1 Cannoli. A lighter or surprising story to end on a sweet note.

You can also listen. The Montreal Weekly is the audio version of Harry's Haul,

My word

Clear writing and storytelling guide every edition so it stays simple, focused, and easy to follow.

Forward thinking drives constant experimentation with new ideas, features, and formats.

Community connection keeps people in the city and those who love it from afar part of the conversation.

Trusted sourcing ensures every story is based on verified information and transparency.

Support for local news helps sustain quality, independent journalism.
