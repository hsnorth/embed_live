Timeline:
The search for Claire Bell
Sunday – Wednesday
Sunday, June 15
Claire Bell, 3, is last seen near Newman Blvd. in Montreal’s LaSalle borough. Her mother later reports her missing at a souvenir shop 60 km away.
Monday, June 16
Police discover a dead Chihuahua, believed to have been with Claire, near Highway 30. The Sûreté du Québec begins searching the Montérégie region.
Tuesday, June 17
Claire’s mother, Rachel Todd, is charged with child abandonment. She appears in court wearing a red shirt and grey blanket. A bail hearing is scheduled.
Wednesday, June 18
Police continue searching near Coteau-du-Lac. The SQ asks residents to check sheds and garages. Garbage collection is paused in nearby towns.
