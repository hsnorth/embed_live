Timeline:

The search for Claire Bell

Scroll right →

Sunday, June 15

Claire Bell, 3, was last seen at 9:45 a.m. near Newman Boulevard in LaSalle, Montreal.

Her mother reported her missing six hours later at a fireworks shop in Coteau-du-Lac.

Monday, June 16

Search expanded to LaSalle, Coteau-du-Lac and near the Ontario border.

A dead Chihuahua, believed to be with Claire, was found near Highway 30.

Tuesday, June 17

  • Morning: Rachel Todd arrested, charged with child abandonment.
  • Afternoon: Todd appears in video court; release opposed.

Searches persisted around Vaudreuil‑Dorion without public volunteers.

Wednesday, June 18

Claire was spotted near Casselman, Ontario, at about 2 p.m.

Ontario Provincial Police found her safe roughly 150 km from Montreal.

Wednesday, June 18 – 3 p.m.

  • Claire Bell is found alive by Ontario Provincial Police.
  • Her father said: “Thank you everyone. Please allow me and my family this time with our girl.”
