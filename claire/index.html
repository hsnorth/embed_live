Timeline: The Search for Claire Bell
2025
A young girl disappears. A city joins the search. This timeline shows the key moments in the first days of the Claire Bell case.
Sunday, June 15
Claire Bell, 3, is last seen near Newman Blvd. in Montreal’s LaSalle borough. Her mother reports her missing at a souvenir shop 60 km away later that day.
Monday, June 16
Police discover a dead Chihuahua near Highway 30, believed to have been with Claire. Search efforts intensify in the Montérégie region.
